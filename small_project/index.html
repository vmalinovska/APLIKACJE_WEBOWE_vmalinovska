Why BLUE?

Blue is worth looking at with a little more attention, even in simple things. For humans, color is often just a word, a mental label, a vision in our eyes. But blue truly surrounds us everywhere, from the plastic bottle sitting on a table to the endless sky on a sunny day. It can be calm and soothing, like the gentle waves of the sea, yet at the same time it can carry a quiet melancholy. This color connects nature and our everyday life, reminding us of vast oceans, distant mountains, and familiar objects. By noticing blue in its many forms, we begin to see the world a little more deeply, finding meaning in what we often take for granted. It’s a subtle yet constant companion in the rhythm of our daily lives.

Veronika Malinovska
